# Appendices: References & Further Reading

This appendix provides a list of key references, academic papers, books, and online resources that have been consulted in the creation of this book. It also suggests further reading for readers who wish to delve deeper into specific topics in physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Key References

### Books

-   **"ROS Robotics Projects"** by Lentin Joseph: A practical guide to building a variety of robotic projects using ROS.
-   **"Programming Robots with ROS"** by Morgan Quigley, Brian Gerkey, William D. Newman: A foundational text for understanding and programming with ROS.
-   **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto: A comprehensive textbook on reinforcement learning.

### Academic Papers

-   **"Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation with Asynchronous Off-Policy Updates"** by Shariq Iqbal, Adam Stooke, and Pieter Abbeel: Pioneering work on training robot policies with deep reinforcement learning.
-   **"Sim-to-Real Transfer of Robotic Control with Dynamics Randomization"** by Xue Bin Peng et al.: A key paper on using domain randomization to bridge the reality gap.
-   **"Learning to Navigate in Complex Environments"** by Alexey Dosovitskiy et al.: Research on end-to-end learning for navigation tasks.

### Online Documentation

-   **ROS 2 Documentation**: The official documentation for the Robot Operating System 2, providing tutorials, concepts, and API references. [docs.ros.org](https://docs.ros.org/)
-   **Gazebo Documentation**: Comprehensive guides and tutorials for the Gazebo simulator. [gazebosim.org/docs](https://gazebosim.org/docs)
-   **NVIDIA Isaac Sim Documentation**: Official documentation for NVIDIA Isaac Sim, including APIs, tutorials, and synthetic data generation guides. [docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/isaacsim/latest/](https://docs.omniverse.nvidia.com/isaacsim/latest/)
-   **OpenAI Whisper Documentation**: Information on using and integrating OpenAI's Whisper ASR model. [openai.com/research/whisper](https://openai.com/research/whisper)

## Further Reading

### Robotics Foundations

-   **"Robot Modeling and Control"** by Mark W. Spong, Seth Hutchinson, M. Vidyasagar: A classic textbook on robot kinematics, dynamics, and control.
-   **"Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control"** by Kevin M. Lynch and Frank C. Park: An excellent modern approach to robotics, available online.

### Artificial Intelligence for Robotics

-   **"Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach"** by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig: A foundational textbook for general AI concepts.
-   **"Deep Learning"** by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville: A comprehensive resource on deep learning theory and applications.

### Advanced Topics

-   **Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)**: Explore journals and conferences such as HRI, RO-MAN, and Frontiers in Robotics and AI.
-   **Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF)**: Research papers on aligning AI models with human values, often relevant for LLM integration in robotics.

This list is not exhaustive but provides a strong starting point for deeper exploration into the fascinating fields covered in this book.
